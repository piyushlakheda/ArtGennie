Mission

These objectives outline ArtGenie's mission to empower artists, promote accessibility, build community, and promote sustainability and ethics in the art market.

The platform aims to create a vibrant community of artists and art lovers, where buyers can discover and purchase authentic, one-of-a-kind paintings, and artists can showcase their talents and gain recognition for their work. ArtGenie also aims to provide a user-friendly and intuitive experience, enabling customers to browse, purchase, and enjoy artwork with ease. Overall, the goal of ArtGenie is to democratize the art market and create a more inclusive and accessible art world for everyone.

How it works?

Select the Artwork

Browse through our curated collection of authentic, one-of-a-kind artworks created by talented artists from around the world. Immerse yourself in a world of artistic expressions, from captivating paintings to mesmerizing sculptures. Find the perfect piece that resonates with your unique taste and style.

Add it to your Cart

Once you've found the artwork that captures your imagination, simply click the 'Add to Cart' button to secure your selection. You can continue exploring our collection or proceed to checkout when you're ready to complete your purchase.

Order given to Artist

We believe in fostering a direct connection between art enthusiasts and the creators. As soon as your order is confirmed, we swiftly communicate it to the artist. They will be notified about your purchase, allowing them to prepare the artwork for shipping.

Order Shipped by experts

To ensure your artwork arrives in pristine condition, our dedicated team of experts meticulously packages each piece with the utmost care. We use industry-standard materials and techniques to safeguard the artwork during transit, protecting its integrity and preserving its beauty

Carefully Packaged and Delivered Safely

On Door Delivery

Sit back, relax, and eagerly anticipate the arrival of your chosen artwork. Our reliable shipping partners will handle the delivery process with professionalism and attention to detail. You can track your package's progress online and expect it to be safely delivered to your designated address
